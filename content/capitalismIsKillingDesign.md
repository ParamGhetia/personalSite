## Capitalism Is Killing Design
The question I seek to answer today is: To what extent does capitalism shape and limit the role of aesthetics and design in everyday life?

The function of our economy in society is incredibly influential. In the context of our current economic system, we place a large emphasis on profit and seeking monetary success. We’re missing even a hint of the drive to create great things that we once had. We’ve sacrificed much in the pursuit of our shallow goal. Art and design are just one of the many victims of this tragedy. 

Our economic system has fundamentally changed the way in which we think. So much so that we don’t even realize the foregone potential of craft. We even fail to imagine a world different from this. 

Today, that is my goal: to prove to you what capitalism has done and show you the wonders spawned by uplifting art and visuals. I hope to detail their true power, more than just an afterthought or vehicle for function.
